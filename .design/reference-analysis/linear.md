# Linear Reference Analysis

Sources: https://linear.app, https://linear.app/blog/how-we-redesigned-the-linear-ui

## Layout
- Dense workspace shell with persistent left navigation and product surfaces framed as working objects, not marketing cards.
- Content rhythm is compact: lists, metadata, status, comments, and panels stay aligned on a tight grid.
- Hero/product sections use live-feeling interface mockups to communicate system behavior.

## Navigation
- Sidebar groups workspace, favorites, projects, and operational views.
- Current context is always visible through selected state, breadcrumbs, and metadata.
- Navigation favors speed and low visual noise over decoration.

## Typography
- Small, precise type scale with strong contrast between titles, metadata, and body copy.
- Labels, issue IDs, counts, and timestamps create product density without clutter.

## Grid
- Uses narrow gutters, strict vertical alignment, and panel-based columns.
- Dashboards combine list columns, detail panes, and timeline surfaces.

## Components
- Issue rows, property chips, activity items, avatars, status pills, side panels, and command-like actions.
- Components feel system-native and consistent.

## Cards
- Cards are understated surfaces with thin borders and minimal shadow.
- Primary information comes first; metadata follows in compact rows.

## Hero
- Product mockups are the hero, showing real workflow states and AI agent collaboration.

## Motion
- Motion is subtle and functional: state changes, hover feedback, reveal, and focus.

## CTA
- CTAs are restrained and practical: sign up, open app, contact, or inspect a product capability.

## Color
- Neutral graphite/dark surfaces with crisp text and restrained accent colors for status and priority.

## Icons
- Small utility icons and avatars; icons support scanning rather than carrying the brand.

## Empty State
- Empty states should teach the workflow and provide the next action.

## Loading
- Loading should be compact and contextual, never a full-screen interruption unless required.

## Visual Philosophy
- High-density, low-noise, fast, operational, AI-native, and obsessively aligned.
