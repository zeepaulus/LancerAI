# Asset And Icon Plan

## Strategy
- Primary assets are original CSS/SVG product visuals in `frontend/src/components/Common/Visuals.jsx`.
- Icons use existing inline SVG system and installed icon packages can be used later, but no external copyrighted assets are copied.
- No stock photos are required for the SaaS product experience.

## Pages
- Landing: original dashboard product mockup, aurora gradients, brand mark. Purpose: communicate AI SaaS product immediately.
- Dashboard: feature icons for voice, CV, matching, reports. Purpose: quick feature discovery.
- Voice Interview/Chat: assistant mark, waveform bars, camera placeholder. Purpose: voice-first interaction clarity.
- Question Bank: assistant mark and question/action cards. Purpose: knowledge-base detail without empty right panel.
- CV Upload/Extraction/Optimization: document intelligence visual. Purpose: make CV parsing tangible.
- Job Matching/Recommendations: match mockup, score ring, job cards. Purpose: role-fit explanation.
- Reports/Interview Report: evaluation score graphic and report panels. Purpose: evidence-backed scanning.
- Profile/Candidate/About: avatar cluster and team cards. Purpose: personal workspace identity.
- Settings/Auth: product mockup and semantic controls. Purpose: trustworthy account/workspace feel.

## Source Register
- `ProductMockupGraphic`, `CVDocumentGraphic`, `EvaluationScoreGraphic`, `CandidateClusterGraphic`: original repo-native SVG/CSS visuals.
- Existing logo/social icons in `frontend/src/assets/Logo`: already present in project; used only in Auth.
- External assets downloaded: none.
